Services

Web Design

We build original designs hand-coded in standards compliant HTML and CSS. We use the latest HTML5 and CSS3 techniques, while continuing to offer support for older browsers. We can help you build a powerful web presence by designing a modern look for your website that fits your business.

Content Management

Content management systems allow you to be in complete control of the content on your website. We can set up your website with software such as Wordpress and Drupal that allows you to keep your website updated yourself, no programming knowledge required.

Web Programming

We can give your website dynamic functionality with web programming technologies such as PHP, Ruby, and Javascript. We can even let your website take phone calls!

eCommerce

Enable your website to accept payments online with powerful online store software. We can set you up with either a preexisting eCommerce package such as Magento, or develop a custom solution that allows you to take payment through third party handlers such as PayPal or Authorize.net.

We like data

One of the largest challenges in web design is creating intuitive methods to interact with data. We have experience managing and organizing data in many different forms.

For example, in our Knight Scheduler web application, we collected and arranged the course listings for SUNY Geneseo, and arranged the courses in a way that was easy and intuitive for students to browse. We also work with statistical data, bringing meaning to what most people see as numbers.

We can help you display your data so that your customers can find what they need quickly and easily.
